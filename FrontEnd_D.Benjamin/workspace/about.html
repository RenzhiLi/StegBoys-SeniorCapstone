About Us

Steganography Group Inc. first formed in CSI4999 Senior Project class at Oakland University in January 2019. We have dedicated this project to understanding the art of steganography and developing a unique solution that detects whether a hidden message is present in an image.

Using machine learning with classification algorithms such as SVM, we have developed an easy-to-use interface that permits anyone to make use of steganography detection; this gives great utility to numerous users. In addition, we have provided adaptability for existing applications which content providers and government officials may use for their respective applications.

Overall, there is great value placed on this application due to its ability to detect whether an image may be hiding a secret message, and there exists the possibility that in the future one may be able to detect more sinister hidden data in an image such as malicious code. For now, we introduce our first product called SDMI which stands for Steganography Detector with Machine Learning.
